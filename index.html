Welcome To Academic Confrontation Site

The Academic Resort is designed to simplify access to semester-teacher-wise course materials, helping reduce the struggle of finding notes, books, slides, and other academic resources. Whether you're looking for senior notes or teacher-provided sheets, everything you need is just a click away.

The site includes content from the 1st through 8th semesters, as well as MBA semesters. Additionally, you can find information about course teachers from various batches, making it easier to locate previous year resources. Best wishes to all Accounting & Information Systems fellows!
