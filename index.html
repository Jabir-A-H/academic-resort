About This Site

The Academic Resort is designed to simplify access to semester-wise course materials, helping reduce the struggle of finding notes, books, slides, and other academic resources. Whether you're looking for senior notes or tutor-provided sheets, everything you need is just a click away.

The archive includes content from the 1st through 8th semesters, as well as MBA semesters. Additionally, you can find information about course tutors from various batches—making it easier to locate previous year resources. Best wishes to all Accounting & Information Systems fellows!
