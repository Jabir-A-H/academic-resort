❓ Questions & Examinations

▼

📝 Class Test Questions

▼

Q1: Define accounting and explain its importance in business decision-making.

Q2: What are the fundamental accounting principles? Explain any three in detail.

Q3: Distinguish between cash basis and accrual basis of accounting with examples.

Q4: Explain the accounting equation and provide examples of transactions affecting it.

🎯 Midterm Questions

▼

Q1: Prepare a trial balance from the following ledger balances and identify any errors.

Q2: Record journal entries for the following transactions and post them to ledger accounts.

Q3: Calculate the missing figure: Assets = 50,000, Liabilities = ?, Owner's Equity = 30,000

Q4: Explain the concept of depreciation and calculate depreciation using straight-line method.

🏆 Final Questions

▼

Q1: Prepare a complete set of financial statements (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement) from the given trial balance.

Q2: Explain the accounting cycle and describe each step with examples.

Q3: Calculate depreciation using straight-line, double-declining balance, and units of production methods.

Q4: Record journal entries for the following transactions and post them to the ledger accounts.

🗂️ Question Banks

▼

📝 Fundamentals of Accounting

Q1: What is accounting? Discuss its nature, scope, and objectives.

Q2: Explain the accounting equation with suitable examples.

Q3: Distinguish between book-keeping and accounting.

📊 Journal and Ledger

Q1: What is a journal? Explain the process of journalizing with examples.

Q2: Define ledger. How is posting done from journal to ledger?

Q3: Prepare journal entries for various business transactions.
