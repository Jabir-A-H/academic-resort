Course Overview

Course Code: 1102

Course Title: Introduction to Business

Semester: 1st Semester

Prerequisites: None

This course introduces fundamental business concepts including business environments, organizational structures, management principles, and basic business operations. Students learn about different types of businesses, stakeholders, and the role of business in society.
