üìã Sample Assignments

Written Assignments

Assignment 1: Write a formal business letter to a supplier inquiring about product specifications and pricing.

Assignment 2: Prepare a 10-minute presentation on "Effective Team Communication" with visual aids.

Assignment 3: Draft a professional email responding to a customer complaint about delayed delivery.

Presentation Projects

Project 1: Create a business proposal presentation for a new product launch (15 minutes).

Project 2: Conduct a mock job interview and provide feedback on communication skills.

Project 3: Demonstrate effective cross-cultural communication through role-play scenarios.

‚ùì Practice Questions

Theory Questions

Q1: Explain the barriers to effective communication and suggest strategies to overcome them.

Q2: Compare formal and informal communication channels in organizations.

Q3: Discuss the importance of non-verbal communication in business settings.

Q4: Analyze the role of technology in modern business communication.

Practical Scenarios

Scenario 1: You need to inform employees about a policy change. Draft an internal memo.

Scenario 2: A client is unhappy with your service. Write a professional response email.

Scenario 3: Prepare talking points for a presentation to potential investors.

Scenario 4: Create an agenda for a team meeting to discuss project deadlines.
