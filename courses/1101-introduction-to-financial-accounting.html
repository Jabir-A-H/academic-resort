1101 Introduction to Financial Accounting

Foundation course covering basic accounting principles, financial statements preparation, and fundamental accounting procedures. Essential for understanding business finance and financial reporting.

📚 Course Information

  • Course Code: 1101
  • Credits: 3 Credit Hours
  • Semester: 1st Semester
  • Prerequisites: None

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand fundamental accounting principles
  • Prepare basic financial statements
  • Apply double-entry bookkeeping system
  • Analyze business transactions

📋 Course Content

  • Introduction to Accounting
  • Accounting Equation & Double Entry
  • Financial Statements Preparation
  • Trial Balance & Adjusting Entries
